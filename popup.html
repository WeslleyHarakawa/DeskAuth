    DeskAuth

    2FA Authenticator for your Desktop

    DeskAuth is a lightweight Chrome extension that lets you access your 2FA codes directly from your desktop, import accounts, organize them into profiles, and generate TOTP codes locally while keeping all data stored only on your device.
